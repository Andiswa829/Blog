2-in-1 Tropicool Breakfast Smoothie

    Ingredients
    For the strawberry band:
    1 cup strawberries, fresh
    For the orange smoothie layer:
    ½ cup mango chunks, frozen
    1/3 cup pineapple chunks, frozen
    ½ cup banana chunks, frozen
    ½ cup full-cream yoghurt
    ¼ cup oats
    ½ cup Cappy Tropical
    3-4 ice blocks
    For the red smoothie layer:
    1 cup strawberries, frozen
    ½ cup full-cream yoghurt
    ¼ cup oats
    ½ cup Cappy Breakfast Blend
    3-4 ice blocks
    To garnish:
    Granola
    2 pineapple slices
    2 strawberries
    Each serving suggestion serves 4
     
    Recipe:
    1. For the strawberry band: Slice strawberries width ways. Press these strawberry pieces against the inside of 2 glass jars – to form a strawberry ‘band’ at the halfway mark of each jar. Set aside.
    2. For the orange layer: Add 1/3 cup frozen mango, 1/3 cup frozen pineapple, 1/3 cup frozen banana chunks, ½ cup yoghurt, 1/4 cup oats, ½ cup Cappy Tropical and 3-4 ice blocks to a blender and blend.
    3. For the red layer: Add 1 cup frozen strawberries, ½ cup yoghurt, 1/4 cup oats, ½ cup Cappy Breakfast Blend and 3-4 ice blocks to a blender and blend. 
    4. Assembly: Pour the orange smoothie mix into 2 glass jars until halfway filled, fill the glasses with the red smoothie mix. Top with a light sprinkle of granola, garnish with a piece of pineapple and a strawberry and ENJOY!
    5. **For best result: chill the jars for 10 – 15 minutes before topping with the red smoothie layer.
